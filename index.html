Our Love Story

Ana and David met through a mutual friend who did her very best to convince Ana that David was a nice chap from Greece...
Ana was very surprised to find he was not Greek, but was, indeed, a geek!

Early on David made Ana walk up Conic Hill twice in one day...the rest is history because Ana's legs were too tired to run away!
Five years later...Ana took David on a stroll in Troon and proposed to him, and he said YES!

Will I be able to stay at the same hotel as the wedding?

Will pets be allowed?
Yes and no. Pets are allowed outside the hotel in the gardens (and on the magnificent walk along the river), but they are not allowed inside where food is served. Some hotel rooms are pet-friendly, so if you would like to bring your little woofer, please request a dog-friendly room when booking.
