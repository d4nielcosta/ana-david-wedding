Our Love Story

Ana and David met through a mutual friend who did her very best to convince Ana that David was a nice chap from Greece...
Ana was very surprised to find he was not Greek, but was, indeed, a geek!

Early on David made Ana walk up Conic Hill twice in one day...the rest is history because Ana's legs were too tired to run away!
Five years later...Ana took David on a stroll in Troon and proposed to him, and he said YES!

The venue

The wedding will be held at Dunkeld House Hotel in Perthshire, Scotland.
The hotel originally served as the Highland retreat for the 7th Duke of Atholl around 1898–1900 and is now a hotel surrounded by many acres of woodland and set along the River Tay.
We believe this hotel perfectly fits our outdoorsy and green thumb personalities and hopefully provides a lovely natural backdrop for the ceremony and photos.

Getting there

The nearest postcode is: PH8 0HX.

From Glasgow

By car

Journey time: 1 hour 45 mins

Take the M8 east, merge onto the M80, then the A9 north towards Perth/ Inverness. Exit at Dunkeld/Birnam and follow signs to Dunkeld House Hotel (approx. 5 minutes from A9).

By train

Journey time: 2 hours

Take the Glasgow Airport Express (500) bus to Glasgow Queen Street Station (15 minutes).
Take a train to Dunkeld & Birnam (most services change at Perth).
Take a taxi ride (5 minutes) from the station to the hotel.

From Edinburgh

By car

Journey time: 1 hour 30 mins

Take the M8 west, then the M80 and A9 North towards Perth/ Inverness. Exit at Dunkeld/Birnam and follow signs to Dunkeld House Hotel (approx. 5 minutes from A9).

By train

Journey time: 2 hours

Take the Airlink 100 bus or Tram to Edinburgh Haymarket Station.
Take a train to Dunkeld & Birnam (most services change at Perth).
Take a taxi ride (5 minutes) from the station to the hotel.

Yes and no. Pets are allowed outside the hotel in the gardens (and on the magnificent walk along the river), but they are not allowed inside where food is served. Some hotel rooms are pet-friendly, so if you would like to bring your little woofer, please request a dog-friendly room when booking.
